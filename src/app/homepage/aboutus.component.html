Parking Go is an effort towards user-friendly driving and organized parking system.There are parking spaces scattered across any city, but are difficult to find when you need them on the go.Parking Go makes the job easy for you, by allowing you to book spots directly from your phone.You can also register slots owned by you, to be used by those in need of parking.
